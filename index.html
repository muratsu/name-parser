If you're doing email marketing, cleaning up data can be a time sink. One of the most common repeated tasks I've seen is grabbing first names from a full name. Google Sheets will soon use AI to split a full name column into two columns, but in my experience, names can come in various forms, and I wanted to have something more robust.

The tool is effortless to use, paste your name list on the left text box, and the tool will automatically print the result on the right text box.
